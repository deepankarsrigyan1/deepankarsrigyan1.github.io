I study ultrafast light–matter control of quantum materials.

I use ultrafast optical, THz, and mid-infrared excitation to drive quantum materials into nonequilibrium regimes where new phases, textures, and functionalities emerge.

By combining nonlinear optical probes, ultrafast spectroscopy, and time-resolved x-ray diffraction and scattering, I investigate ferroic superlattices, magnetic heterostructures, 2D materials, and spin-chiral systems, revealing how symmetry, topology, and dynamics intertwine far from equilibrium.

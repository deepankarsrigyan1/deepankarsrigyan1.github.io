I am a scientist studying the physics of materials, using light to interrogate and manipulate quantum materials far from equilibrium.

My research focuses on ultrafast nonequilibrium phases, ferroic textures, and symmetry-breaking dynamics using pump–probe optics, nonlinear SHG, and time-resolved x-ray scattering.
